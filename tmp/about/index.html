  • Bio
  • PROFESSIONAL AFFILIATIONS

Creating a beautiful smile requires both technical excellence and an artistic eye. Dr. David R Thurman, DDS combines both disciplines to produce a unique result based on your individual characteristics and needs.

David’s commitment to excellence includes participation in numerous hours of continuing education courses related to dental implants, cutting edge technology and advanced dental techniques.

Dr. Thurman was born and raised in Montana. He received his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from The Ohio State University – College of Dentistry in 2007 and has dedicated his professional life to providing his patients with outstanding dental care.

Dr. Thurman lives in beautiful Denver, Colorado where he enjoys spending time with friends, exploring the nearby Rocky Mountains, road biking and rooting for local sports teams.
